FAQ

Everything You Might Want to Know

Which services usually need a consultation first?

Color corrections, balayage, bridal packages, and multi-service appointments are best preceded by a quick consultation so the team can estimate time, pricing, and preparation.

Can I request a same-day booking?

Same-day appointments may be available for simpler services, but premium time slots fill quickly. Booking ahead gives you the best chance of getting your ideal stylist and schedule.

What should I bring or mention before my appointment?

If you're booking color, bridal, or skincare services, it helps to mention previous treatments, allergies, inspiration photos, or timing needs so the salon can prepare properly.
